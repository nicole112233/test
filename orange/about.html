OrangePay is a payment service provider that serves its clients, the merchants, by taking care of the processing of payments made between the merchant and its customers. The processing of payments entails the management of the cash flows in a transaction from the customer making the purchase, up until the merchant receiving the amount on their accounts.
As a Dutch corporation, headquatered at the Zuidas of Amsterdam, The Netherlands. OrangePay is founded as an independent entity by its partners with 3 different business backgrounds, know-how, and expertise, which are bundled together in OrangePay.
We are mostly focused on transactions between Chinese and European businesses and their customers.
We have partners located in China. With representatives in both China and the Netherlands, communication, customer relations and accessibility are enhanced.
Within OrangePay, we have a highly experienced management team with knowledge and expertise on the areas of payment technology, compliance, business administration. The management team is also familiar with both Chinese and European market and culture. And management and partners have an extensive network that crosses different industries and even countries.
